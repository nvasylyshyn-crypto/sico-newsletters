A Word from the Group CEO

The first half of 2025 has been shaped by a series of global challenges. Geopolitical tensions, trade disruptions, and fluctuating oil prices have created a complex environment for investors and markets alike.

These developments have tested resilience across the board and reminded us of the importance of staying focused on long-term fundamentals.

At SICO, we remain committed to guiding our clients through uncertainty with clarity and confidence. Our focus on research, asset management, trades execution, securities safekeeping and advisory services allows us to deliver multilayered value through insight and partnership.

This year also marks a significant milestone for us—SICO’s 30th anniversary. For three decades, we have proudly served as a trusted financial partner in the region, navigating change, embracing innovation, and staying true to our mission of delivering excellence. This anniversary is not just a celebration of our past, but a reaffirmation of our commitment to the future.

For the second half of the year, we are excited to deliver more opportunities for our clients and create new ones taking advantage of our regional presence, while we remain attentive to global risk impact on the region.

Thank you for your continued trust.

Najla Al-Shirawi

Group Chief Executive Officer
